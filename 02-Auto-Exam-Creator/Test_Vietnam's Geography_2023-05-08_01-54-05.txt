

Q1. What is the capital of Vietnam? 
A. Ho Chi Minh City 
B. Hanoi 
C. Da Nang 
D. Saigon 

Correct Answer: B. Hanoi 


Q2. What is the largest city in Vietnam? 
A. Ho Chi Minh City 
B. Hanoi 
C. Da Nang 
D. Saigon 

Correct Answer: A. Ho Chi Minh City 


Q3. Which river runs through the center of Vietnam? 
A. Mekong River 
B. Red River 
C. Ma River 
D. Hoang River 

Correct Answer: A. Mekong River 


Q4. What is the highest mountain in Vietnam? 
A. Mount Fansipan 
B. Mount Ngoc Linh 
C. Mount Animas 
D. Mount Fako 

Correct Answer: B. Mount Ngoc Linh
