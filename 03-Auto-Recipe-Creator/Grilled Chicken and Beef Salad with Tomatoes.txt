

Recipe Title: Grilled Chicken and Beef Salad with Tomatoes

Ingredients: 
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 2-4 ounces of beef, cubed
- 2 cups of salad greens
- 4 tomatoes, diced

Instructions:
1. Preheat a grill or a large skillet to medium-high heat.
2. Rub the chicken breasts with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
3. Place the chicken on the grill or skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, until cooked through.
4. Remove the chicken from the heat and set aside.
5. In the same skillet or grill, add the remaining olive oil and the beef cubes.
6. Cook the beef cubes until lightly browned and cooked through.
7. Place the salad greens in a large bowl.
8. Add the grilled chicken, beef cubes, and diced tomatoes to the bowl, and toss together.
9. Serve the salad warm or chilled. Enjoy!
